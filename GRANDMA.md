Hi Grandma! 👋
Now that you’re finally done taking care of everyone else and slogging away at work, it is high time you lived life to the fullest! I built this app, Vibe Voyager, to be your personal adventure companion.

What is this?
Think of this as a magic window on your computer. Inside, there is a smart assistant named Lumi. She is like a world-travel expert and a professional chef mixed into one.

How to use it:
There are two main buttons (we call them "Tabs") at the top:

✈️ The Trip Tab: If you want to see the world, Lumi will help you plan everything. She’ll pick a beautiful destination based on how you want to feel, tell you exactly what cute outfits to pack, and even put the dates on your calendar for you.

🏠 The Home Tab: If your body is feeling a bit tired or you just want to relax, you can still "travel" from your favorite armchair. Lumi will give you a fancy recipe to cook, a new hobby to try, and music to listen to so your living room feels like a five-star hotel.

The Rules of the Game:
Lumi is very smart, but she has her limits (and her feet are firmly on the ground):

She isn't a doctor: Please don't go asking her for medical advice—stick to your real doctor for that!

She stays on Earth: She won't plan a trip to Jupiter or the lost city of Atlantis.

She likes peace: She’s not going to suggest a vacation to North Korea. We want you safe and happy!

I built this using some complicated "AI" tools, but all you need to know is that it’s here to make sure your next chapter is the most fun one yet. Love you!
